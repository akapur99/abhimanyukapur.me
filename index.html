A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Abhimanyu, but you can call me Abhi. I'm a sophomore, studying Computer Science and Psychology, at Dartmouth College from Mumbai, India.
I'm currently looking for a Summer 2019 internship, please reach out if you have an opportunity you think I'd be a fit for!

On campus, I'm currently working for the Political Violence Lab to perform sentiment analysis on twitter data relating to the drug war in the Philippines using Python, though my favourite language is Java. I'm also an incoming TA for a foundational CS course (Winter '19') and an Apprentice Developer at the DALI Lab. I can also do basic work with Matlab, Figma and Photoshop CC 2017. What I enjoy most about development and design, is between empathy-driven user research and the post-implementation feeling of having created something that will have an impact.

In my free time you can find me practicing the saxophone, trying to write poetry, gaming or dancing!
